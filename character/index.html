Biography

Mr. Meeseeks first appeared in the fifth episode of the first season of the American adult animated science-fiction sitcom Rick and Morty. Meeseeks are humanoid creatures created through a gadget called the Meeseeks box, where each Meeseeks is given an objective to complete for which it will go to any length to fufill. After a Meeseeks' purpose is complete, it will vanish. If Meeseeks are unable to complete their purpose they will continue to live. When a Meeseeks stays alive for too long, it will continue to lose sanity, taking more drastic measures to complete its purpose.

Traits

  • At the start and end of every sentence Mr. Meeseeks will say his catchphrase: "I'm Mr. Meeseeks! Look at me!"
  • As Meeseeks continues to age, it will start to grow grey patches, stubbles, and appear more deranged.
  • A Meeseeks can create another Meeseeks using the Meeseeks box.
  • Meeseeks have white color blood.
